3.	AC Dashboard
Prompt 1: 
Please rename the “National Dashboard” to “AC Dashboard.” And the subheading should also be renamed as “Monitor your cooperative’s performance, assets, and progress in one place.”


Prompt 2:
The “AC Dashboard” should consist of the following widgets and be arranged according to the priority level.
1.	A Total Membership Display that displays total active members, new members enrolled in the current calendar year, and member dropout rate as a percentage. 
(Priority Level – Critical)
2.	A Member Demographics Visualization that displays member demographics, including gender distribution (M/F/Other), age group bands, and distribution by crop type and livestock category using chart components.
(Priority Level – High)
3.	A Business Plan Status Widget that displays the cooperative's current business plan status: Submitted / Under Review / Approved / Rejected; current implementation progress as a percentage; list of upcoming milestones with due dates; and red/amber alerts for activities that are overdue.
(Priority Level – Critical)
4.	An Asset Overview Widget that displays the count of active assets, breakdown by condition (Good/Fair/Poor/Out of Service), and a list of assets overdue for maintenance.
(Priority Level – High)
5.	A Knowledge & Training Metrics that show the number of knowledge materials received, the activity completion indicator, and member participation statistics for training sessions.
(Priority Level – Medium)
6.	A Graduation Status Indicator that displays the cooperative's current graduation stage (Starter / Developing / Expanding/ Advanced) and date of last assessment.
(Priority Level – High)
